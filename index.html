Kimmi Kaiy

Kimmi Kaiy is a dynamic UK-based content creator and stand-up comedian known for her sharp, unfiltered dark humor and high-energy personality. She has successfully built a massive social media following across TikTok and Instagram, where she shares viral sketches, listener confessions, and lifestyle content. Beyond her digital presence, she is a regular on the comedy circuit, performing at renowned clubs and hosting her own popular podcast.

Jen Jenivive

Jen Jenivive is a UK-based author who specializes in creating adult parody books disguised as innocent children’s stories. She has built a massive social media following by using heavy sexual innuendo and “blind reaction” videos to market her provocative titles. Her brand centers on a cheeky, subversive sense of humor that turns nostalgic aesthetics into explicit comedy for a mature audience.
